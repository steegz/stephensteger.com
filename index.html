About

Stephen is an award winning Product Designer with more than 10 years of experience taking high-volume, consumer electronics products from design concept through mass-production.

He is currently a Product Design lead for Snapchat Spectacles, and his past clients and employers include Ammunition, Beats By Dre, Google, Lyft, Ember, Incase, Intel, and Polaroid.

He is a Founding Partner of RocketFuel, a team of angel investors which has made more than 20 seed and early stage investments in the blockchain and distributed ledger space.

Stephen has a B.S.E in Mechanical Engineering from MIT and is an inventor on more than 18 utility and design patents.
